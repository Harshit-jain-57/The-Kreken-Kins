"A world where everyone is connected to the ocean’s wonders and works together to preserve its fragile beauty. Our mission is to uncover the mysteries of the deep sea, inspire curiosity, and drive action to protect our oceans. We envision a world where the beauty and importance of marine ecosystems are understood and respected by all. WHY
THE OCEAN
MATTERS
The ocean generates over 50% of the oxygen we breathe and absorbs nearly a third of the CO₂ we produce, acting as Earth’s life support system. It regulates global weather, sustains billions through food and livelihoods, and harbors 80% of our planet’s biodiversity. Protecting the ocean isn’t optional—it’s essential for the survival of all life on Earth
